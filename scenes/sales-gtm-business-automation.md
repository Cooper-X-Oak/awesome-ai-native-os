# Sales / GTM / Business Automation

## Business Core Abstraction

Turn leads, accounts, company signals, webpage data, sales emails, GTM rules, and cross-app workflows into sales actions, enriched lists, scores, automations, and outreach context.

## What To Compare

| Operating route | Example projects | Difference |
| --- | --- | --- |
| table-first GTM OS | Clay | Lead/account table is the main operating space. |
| prospect research / sales intelligence | Persana, Apollo, Common Room | Produces sales context around people, companies, accounts, and signals. |
| workflow/browser automation | Bardeen, Zapier Agents, Browse AI | Produces automation recipes, browser actions, and extracted data. |
| sales content / sequence | Lavender, Regie.ai | Focuses on email/sequence production; OS boundary narrower. |
| revenue platform | 6sense | Enterprise revenue platform; not first-priority AI-native OS. |
