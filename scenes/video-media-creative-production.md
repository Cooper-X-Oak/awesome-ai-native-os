# Video / Media / Creative Production

## Business Core Abstraction

Turn scripts, prompts, assets, characters, voices, creative directions, timelines, and production stages into publishable media: videos, clips, avatar videos, audio, ads, or multimodal creative assets.

## What To Compare

This scene is valuable because the business goal can be similar while the architecture is radically different.

| Operating route | Example projects | Difference |
| --- | --- | --- |
| pipeline-first | OpenMontage | Splits media production into stages, tools, agent skills, and intermediate artifacts. |
| node-canvas-first | OpenFlow | Uses nodes and edges to organize multimodal generation. |
| timeline/transcript-first | Descript | Uses transcript and timeline as the editing surface. |
| creative-workspace-first | Krea, Runway | Centers iteration around creative direction, variants, generation, and enhancement. |
| single-purpose workflow | OpusClip, HeyGen, Synthesia | Optimized for clips, avatar videos, localization, training videos, etc. |
