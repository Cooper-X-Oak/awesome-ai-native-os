# Data Analytics / BI / Financial Analysis

## Business Core Abstraction

Turn datasets, spreadsheets, databases, metrics, SEC filings, commerce data, or business questions into analysis, charts, dashboards, insights, briefs, or data apps.

## What To Compare

| Operating route | Example projects | Difference |
| --- | --- | --- |
| spreadsheet-first | Quadratic | The grid is the operating space for AI, code, data, and outputs. |
| analyst workspace | Julius | Analysis starts from user questions and produces charts/reports. |
| notebook/data app | Hex, DataButton | Data, code, narrative, and app output are mixed. |
| BI / metric room | Databricks Genie, Power BI, Tableau Pulse | Enterprise metrics, dashboards, semantic layers, and insights. |
| finance/research analyst | LedgerLens, SignalBrief | Specialized analyst workflows. |
