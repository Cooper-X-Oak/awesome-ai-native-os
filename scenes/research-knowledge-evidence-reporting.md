# Research / Knowledge / Evidence Reporting

>
> 生产有依据的回答、报告、文献综述和长文研究。

## English

### Business Core Abstraction

Turn questions, topics, source collections, literature, web materials, or knowledge bases into grounded answers, notes, evidence tables, reports, outlines, or long-form research artifacts.

### What To Compare

| Axis | Questions / Difference |
| --- | --- |
| source-grounded notebook | NotebookLM constrains AI work with user-provided sources. |
| research-to-writing pipeline | STORM stages research, outline, and writing. |
| autonomous web research | GPT Researcher and Deep Research search, synthesize, and report. |
| literature evidence table | Elicit focuses on paper search, extraction, and comparison. |
| answer/search product | Consensus and Perplexity are strong at retrieval and answers but have weaker OS boundaries. |

## 中文

### 业务核心抽象

把问题、topic、来源集合、文献、网页资料或知识库，变成有依据的回答、笔记、证据表、报告、outline 或长文研究产物。

### 这一章要比较什么

| 维度 | 问题 / 差异 |
| --- | --- |
| source-grounded notebook | NotebookLM 用用户提供的 sources 限定 AI 工作边界。 |
| research-to-writing pipeline | STORM 把研究、outline 和写作分阶段。 |
| autonomous web research | GPT Researcher 和 Deep Research 搜索、综合并产出报告。 |
| literature evidence table | Elicit 聚焦论文搜索、信息抽取和比较。 |
| answer/search product | Consensus 和 Perplexity 检索/回答强，但 OS 边界较弱。 |
