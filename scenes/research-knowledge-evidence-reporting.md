# Research / Knowledge / Evidence Reporting

## Business Core Abstraction

Turn questions, topics, source collections, literature, web materials, or knowledge bases into grounded answers, notes, evidence tables, reports, outlines, or long-form research artifacts.

## What To Compare

| Operating route | Example projects | Difference |
| --- | --- | --- |
| source-grounded notebook | NotebookLM | User-provided sources constrain AI answers and notes. |
| research-to-writing pipeline | STORM | Research, outline, and writing are staged into a report process. |
| autonomous web research | GPT Researcher, Deep Research | The system searches the web, synthesizes, and produces a report. |
| literature evidence table | Elicit | Focuses on paper search, extraction, and comparison. |
| answer/search product | Consensus, Perplexity | Strong retrieval and answers, weaker durable OS boundary. |
