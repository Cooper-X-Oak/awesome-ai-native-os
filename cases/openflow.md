## English

### One-Line Positioning

Node-canvas-first multimodal AI production OS.

### Business Scene

Video / media / creative production.

### Core Business Object

Multimodal generation graph.

### Production Artifact

Images, video, audio, text, or reusable generative pipeline.

### Operating Space

Node canvas.

### AI Operation Model

Compose, route, generate, and execute pipeline nodes.

### Human Control Model

Human organizes nodes, edges, parameters, and generated outputs.

### State And Memory

Graph state, node parameters, generation results, and pipeline configuration.

### Delivery Boundary

Generated multimodal assets or reusable pipeline.

### Reusable Pattern

Represent AI production as an editable graph where humans control structure and AI operates nodes.
