# Clay

## One-Line Positioning

Table-first GTM/business OS.

## Business Scene

Sales / GTM / business automation.

## Core Business Object

Lead/account table with enrichments, signals, and workflows.

## Production Artifact

Lead list, account intelligence, enrichment table, or GTM workflow.

## Operating Space

Table workspace.

## AI Operation Model

Enrich, score, research, personalize, and automate.

## Human Control Model

Human configures columns, data sources, rules, enrichment steps, and reviews results.

## State And Memory

Table rows, enrichment history, workflow state, data sources, and generated context.

## Delivery Boundary

Lead/account intelligence and GTM workflow output.

## Reusable Pattern

Use a table as the operating surface for AI-powered business data enrichment and action.
