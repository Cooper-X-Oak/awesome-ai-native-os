# Descript

## One-Line Positioning

Transcript/timeline-first AI-native media editing OS.

## Business Scene

Video / media / creative production.

## Core Business Object

Video/audio project with transcript and timeline.

## Production Artifact

Edited video or audio.

## Operating Space

Transcript + timeline editor.

## AI Operation Model

Transcribe, edit, generate, clean, and publish.

## Human Control Model

Human edits through transcript and timeline, reviews output, and exports.

## State And Memory

Transcript, timeline, media assets, edits, and versions.

## Delivery Boundary

Finished video/audio output.

## Reusable Pattern

Convert opaque media into text-addressable and timeline-addressable objects AI can help edit.
