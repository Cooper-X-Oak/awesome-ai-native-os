### One-Line Positioning

Source-grounded notebook/research OS.

### Business Scene

Research / knowledge / evidence reporting.

### Core Business Object

Source set, notebook, notes, questions, and citations.

### Production Artifact

Grounded answers, notes, briefs, and research summaries.

### Operating Space

Notebook + source workspace.

### AI Operation Model

Retrieve, summarize, cite, answer, and organize source-grounded knowledge.

### Human Control Model

Human selects sources, asks questions, checks citations, and curates outputs.

### State And Memory

Source set, notebook state, notes, citations, and conversation context.

### Delivery Boundary

Grounded answer / note / brief.

### Reusable Pattern

Constrain AI work with a user-controlled source boundary and make citation state visible.
