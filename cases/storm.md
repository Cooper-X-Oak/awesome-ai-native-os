# STORM

## One-Line Positioning

Research-to-writing pipeline OS.

## Business Scene

Research / knowledge / evidence reporting.

## Core Business Object

Topic, collected sources, outline, draft, and citations.

## Production Artifact

Long-form article or research report.

## Operating Space

Research-writing pipeline.

## AI Operation Model

Research, outline, simulate interviews, synthesize, and write.

## Human Control Model

Human sets topic, inspects outline/report, and evaluates citations or structure.

## State And Memory

Collected sources, outline, draft, citations, and pipeline stages.

## Delivery Boundary

Long-form article / report.

## Reusable Pattern

Convert research writing into staged pipeline artifacts instead of a single generation step.
